# 🏛️ Room: Governance & Regulation
**Platform:** [TryHackMe](https://tryhackme.com/p/solomon.mwaura) | **Category:** GRC & Strategy

## 📖 Executive Summary
This room explores the critical frameworks that align cybersecurity with organizational goals. As a professional with a **BCom background**, I focused on how risk management and compliance (GRC) serve as the backbone of enterprise security.

## 🛠️ Key Frameworks & Regulations
* **NIST SP 800-53:** Explored the catalog of security and privacy controls for federal information systems.
* **ISO/IEC 27001:** Analyzed the international standard for managing information security.
* **GDPR & HIPAA:** Reviewed the legal requirements for handling Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and Protected Health Information (PHI).

## 🚀 Lab Objectives & Outcomes
* **Control Mapping:** Practiced mapping technical safeguards to regulatory requirements.
* **Policy Hierarchy:** Differentiated between Policies (the 'what'), Standards (the 'requirements'), and Procedures (the 'how').
* **Risk Assessment:** Learned to categorize assets based on the impact of a potential breach.

## 💡 Professional Alignment
This lab reinforces my work in **Moringa Labs 10 (GDPR)** and **11 (HIPAA)**, demonstrating my ability to navigate complex regulatory landscapes while maintaining a strong security posture.
