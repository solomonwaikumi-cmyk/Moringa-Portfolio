# 🏥 Lab: ISO 27001 & HIPAA Risk Assessment
**Category:** GRC / Information Security Management | **Frameworks:** ISO 27001, NIST, HIPAA

## 📖 Project Overview
Conducted a formal risk assessment for *SecureHealth Inc.*, a healthcare provider transitioning to cloud storage. This project involved identifying critical assets, quantifying risks using ISO 27001 methodology, and mapping technical controls to HIPAA regulatory requirements.

## 🎯 Methodology: ISO 27001 Risk Scoring
I applied a standardized **Impact vs. Likelihood** scoring system to prioritize threats to Protected Health Information (PHI). 

### **Critical Risks Identified:**
* **Unauthorized Data Access:** High-impact risk due to potential HIPAA violations and patient privacy breaches.
* **Insecure Cloud Configuration:** Identified vulnerabilities in the cloud-based infrastructure that could lead to data exposure.
* **Lack of Audit Trails:** Found gaps in logging and monitoring, complicating incident response and forensic efforts.

## 🛡️ Mitigation & Control Recommendations
Mapped identified risks to specific ISO 27001 controls and HIPAA safeguards:
* **Administrative:** Enforced periodic risk reviews and employee security awareness training.
* **Technical:** Recommended AES-256 encryption for data at rest and multi-factor authentication (MFA) for all administrative access.
* **Physical:** Validated cloud provider data center security against ISO 27001 Annex A controls.

## 📁 Evidence & Deliverables
* **HIPAA Risk Register:** A detailed matrix quantifying risk levels and documenting mitigation owners.
* **ISO 27001 Report:** Final executive summary and technical recommendations.
